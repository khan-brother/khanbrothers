Our Services

We offer a comprehensive suite of import and export services tailored to meet your business needs. Our experienced team ensures seamless and efficient handling of your goods, from origin to destination.

Customs Clearance

Our expert team handles all aspects of customs clearance with 30+ years of experience. We specialize in Customs Classifications, SRO's, C.G.O/Public Notices/Notifications, solving customs issues promptly, and clearing stuck shipments. Fully registered with Customs House Karachi and Chamber of Commerce.

Freight Forwarding

Comprehensive freight services for all shipment types (FCL/LCL) through Karachi's major ports QICT, KICT, KGTL, KPT, AICT, BOML, SAPT, PAK SHAHEEN, and Karachi Airport to worldwide destinations. We handle both air and sea freight with professional coordination.

Used Car Clearance

We provide hassle-free used car clearance services, ensuring smooth customs procedures and quick delivery. Our team handles all documentation and regulations, saving you time and effort.

Used Parts

Khan Brothers offers reliable sourcing and supply of quality used auto parts. Whether for maintenance or replacement, we connect you with trusted parts at competitive prices.

Machineries

From industrial to commercial needs, we specialize in the import and clearance of used machinery. Our efficient process ensures safe handling, compliance, and timely delivery to your destination.
