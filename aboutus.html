About KHAN BROTHERS

Our Story

KHAN BROTHERS has been providing exceptional customs clearing, forwarding, and shipping services for over 30 years. We specialize in handling all types of shipments (FCL and LCL) through Karachi's major ports including QICT, KICT, KGTL, KPT, AICT, BOML, SAPT, PAK SHAHEEN, and Karachi Airport to worldwide destinations. Our experienced team ensures smooth customs clearance and timely delivery for all our clients.

Our Mission

To provide professional, hardworking, and proactive customs clearing and forwarding services while maintaining flexible solutions tailored to each customer's specific needs. We operate as an extension of our customers' businesses, ensuring consistent, timely, and accurate communication.

Our Values

* Professionalism: Adhering to industry standards with customized solutions
* Expertise: Specialized knowledge in Customs Classifications, SRO's, C.G.O/Public Notices
* Reliability: Solving customs issues and clearing stuck shipments promptly
* Customer Focus: Flexible approaches tailored to each client's requirements
* Integrity: Fully licensed and registered with Customs House and Chamber of Commerce
